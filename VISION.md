# Vision

Healthcare is entering a period of rapid AI transformation.

Every week brings new research, regulations, startups, products, and technological breakthroughs. Keeping up is becoming increasingly difficult.

My goal is to combine my experience in product and program leadership with AI to help healthcare organizations understand, evaluate and adopt AI in a practical and responsible way.

I am not approaching this as a clinician or machine learning researcher.

My perspective is product strategy, implementation, operational readiness and organizational change.

Over the coming years I want to build useful AI tools, openly document what I learn, and become a trusted voice explaining what new AI developments actually mean for healthcare organizations.
